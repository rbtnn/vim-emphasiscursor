Commands                                               *emphasiscursor-commands*

                                                         *:EmphasisCursor*
:EmphasisCursor [-count={c}] [-msec={m}] [-zindex={z}] [-highlight={h}]

		'count'		blink count
				(default: 10)
		'msec'		milliseconds in a blink
				(default: 50)
		'zindex'	zindex of the popupwin or floating window
				(default: 100)
		'highlight'	highlight of the popupwin or floating window
				(default: 'Search')
